Aries

March 21–April 19 They naturally take charge because they are good at initiating new projects. They can also be impatient, but are naturally active and don't like to waste time.

Taurus

April 20–May 20 They're very down to earth and loyal. Sometimes, they also make the best people to go to for advice.

Geminis

May 21–June 21 They are perceptive, analytical, and often very funny. They have an unreserved, childlike curiosity, always asking new questions.

Cancer

June 22–July 22 Primarily known for being emotional, nurturing, and highly intuitive, as well as being sensitive and sometimes insecure..

Leo

July 23–August 22 Leos are known for their generosity of time, energy, respect, and money. As a result, Leos are attracted to others.

Virgo

August 23–September 22 Virgos are logical, practical, and systematic in their approach to life.

Libra

September 23–October 23 Libras are compassionate and empathetic people. They are willing to go out of their way to help others.

Scorpio

October 23–November 21 Scorpios don't see or establish limitations in their life, and they won't let anybody tell them that they can't have what they desire.

Sagittarius

November 22 - December 21 Sagittarius people are faithful, intelligent, forceful, and sympathetic. They are one-of-a-kind, gifted, and wise beyond their years.

Capricorn

December 21– January 19 Capricorns are practical, determined, diligent, attention-focused and good with money. They enjoy making things work harder, run better, grow bigger.

Aquarius

January 21– February 19 Aquarians are highly intellectual and creative, Walker says. Marked by independence, they don't like to be instructed what to do.

Pisces

March 21– April 19 Pisces are highly creative and imaginative, Walker reports, as well as compassionate and loving. Their emotional sensitivity factor is high.
